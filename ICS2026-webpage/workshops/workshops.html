ACM International Conference on Supercomputing 2026

6-9 July 2026 Belfast, Northern Ireland, United Kingdom

Workshops (June 8th)

Workshops will be held in the Warnock Engineering Building (WEB map) at University of Utah on June 8th 2025.

ICS2025 will hold the following workshops:

All-day workshops:

  • MCCSys: 2nd Workshop on Memory-Centric Computing Systems
    Location: WEB 1230

  • SFWQHPC: 1st International Workshop on Software Frameworks for Integrating Quantum and HPC Ecosystems
    Location: WEB 1250

Afternoon-only workshop:

  • SHDA: 2nd Workshop on Software and Hardware Co-design of Deep Learning Systems in Future Architectures
    Location: WEB 2230
